## Gradient

$$\nabla f = \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\hat{i} +
\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\hat{j} +
\frac{\partial f}{\partial z}\hat{k}$$

## Normal unit vector

$$\hat{n} = \frac{\nabla f}{|f|}$$

## Differential normal area

$$d \vec{A} = dA \cdot \hat{n}$$

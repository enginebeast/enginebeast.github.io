Flux of an electric field

fold/unfold $$ \begin{flalign*} \Phi_E = \oint\vec{E}\cdot d\vec{A} && \end{flalign*} $$

Gauss' law

fold/unfold $$ \begin{flalign*} \epsilon_0\Phi = q_{enc} \\ \Rightarrow\epsilon_0\oint\vec{E}\cdot d\vec{A} = q_{enc} && \end{flalign*} $$

--- title: "Gauss' law" categories: - physics_note use_math: true ---

Flux of an electric field

fold/unfold $$ \begin{flalign*} \Phi_E = \oint\vec{E}\cdot d\vec{A} && \end{flalign*} $$

Gauss' law

fold/unfold $$ \begin{flalign*} & \epsilon_0\Phi = q_{enc} \\ & \Rightarrow\epsilon_0\oint\vec{E}\cdot d\vec{A} = q_{enc} && \end{flalign*} $$ If \(q_{enc}\) is positive, a net flux heads to the outside. And if \(q_{enc}\) is negative, a net flux heads to the inside.

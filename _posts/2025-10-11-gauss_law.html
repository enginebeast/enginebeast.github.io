Flux of an electric field

fold/unfold $$ \begin{flalign*} \Phi_E = \oint\vec{E}\cdot d\vec{A} && \end{flalign*} $$ About top or bottom of cylindal, $$ \begin{flalign*} d\vec{A} = rdrd\theta\hat{z} && \end{flalign*} $$ About side of cylindal, $$ \begin{flalign*} d\vec{A} = rd\theta dz\hat{r} && \end{flalign*} $$ About surface of sphere, $$ \begin{flalign*} d\vec{A} = r^2 \sin\phi d\theta d\phi \hat{r} && \end{flalign*} $$

Gauss' law

fold/unfold $$ \begin{flalign*} & \epsilon_0\Phi = q_{enc} \\ & \Rightarrow\epsilon_0\oint\vec{E}\cdot d\vec{A} = q_{enc} && \end{flalign*} $$ If \(q_{enc}\) is positive, a net flux heads to the outside. And if \(q_{enc}\) is negative, a net flux heads to the inside.

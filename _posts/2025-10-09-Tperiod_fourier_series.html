Definition of Fourier series

fold/unfold $$ \begin{flalign*} f(t) = a_0 + \sum^\infty_{n=1} a_n \cos(n\omega_0 t) + \sum^\infty_{n=1} b_n \sin(n\omega_0 t) && \end{flalign*} $$

Formula of Fourier coefficients

fold/unfold $$ \begin{flalign*} & a_0 = \frac{1}{T}\int_0^T f(t)\,dt \\ \\ & a_n = \frac{2}{T}\int_0^T f(t)\cos(n\omega_0 t)\,dt \\ \\ & b_n = \frac{2}{T}\int_0^T f(t)\sin(n\omega_0 t)\,dt && \end{flalign*} $$

Fourier coefficients for even function

fold/unfold $$ \begin{flalign*} & a_0 = \frac{2}{T}\int^{T/2}_0 f(t)\,dt \\ \\ & a_n = \frac{4}{T}\int^{T/2}_0 f(t)\cos(n\omega_0 t)\,dt \\ \\ & b_n = 0 && \end{flalign*} $$

Fourier coefficients for odd function

fold/unfold $$ \begin{flalign*} & a_0 = 0 \\ \\ & a_n = 0 \\ \\ & b_n = \frac{4}{T}\int^{T/2}_0 f(t)\sin(n\omega_0 t)\,dt && \end{flalign*} $$

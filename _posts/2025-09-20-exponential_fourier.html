--- title: "Exponential Fourier series" categories: - math_note use_math: true comments: true ---

Definition Fourier series

$$ \begin{flalign*} & f(t) = \sum^\infty_{-\infty}c_ne^{jn\omega t} && \end{flalign*} $$

Formula of Fourier coefficient

$$ \begin{flalign*} c_n = \int^{t_0+T}_{t_0}f(t)e^{-jn\omega t}dt && \end{flalign*} $$

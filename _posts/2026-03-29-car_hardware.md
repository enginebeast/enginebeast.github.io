- Foam core: materials for car body
- Arduino board: Brain of this project
- Motor driver
- Motor * 4
- Plastic wheel * 4
- Bluetooth module
- 9V battery * 2: One is power for arduino, and another is power for motor driver.
- Battery case
- Bolts and nuts

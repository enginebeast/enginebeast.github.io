Capacitor in parallel

fold/unfold In paraller circuit, all voltages are equal. $$ \begin{flalign*} V = V_1 = V_2 = \cdot\cdot\cdot = V_n && \end{flalign*} $$ $$ \begin{flalign*} q = \sum^n_{i=1}q_i && \end{flalign*} $$ $$ \begin{flalign*} & \frac{q}{V} = \sum^n_{i_1}\frac{q_i}{V} \\& \Rightarrow C = \sum^n_{i=1}C_i && \end{flalign*} $$

Capacitor in series

fold/unfold $$ \begin{flalign*} q = q_1 = q_2 = \cdot\cdot\cdot = q_n && \end{flalign*} $$ $$ \begin{flalign*} V = \sum^n_{i=1}V_i && \end{flalign*} $$ $$ \begin{flalign*} & \frac{V}{q} = \sum^n_{i_1}\frac{V_i}{q} \\& \Rightarrow \frac{1}{C} = \sum^n_{i=1}\frac{1}{C_i} && \end{flalign*} $$

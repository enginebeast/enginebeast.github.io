Definition of electric fields

fold/unfold $$ \begin{flalign*} \vec{E} = \frac{\vec{F}}{q_0} && \end{flalign*} $$ Where, \(q_0\) is test charge which become the standard of electric fields.

By the Coulomb's law, we can get the following equation. $$ \begin{flalign*} \vec{E} = \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0}\frac{qq_0}{r^2}\hat{r}\frac{1}{q_0} = \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0}\frac{q}{r^2}\hat{r} && \end{flalign*} $$
